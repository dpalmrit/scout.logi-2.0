The Process

to full intel

01

Upload the Footage

Drop any video file — full match or a clip. MP4, MOV, most formats. No professional camera needed. Sideline phone footage is enough.

Any Camera Works Phone, tripod, coach cam, or stadium feed — if the player is visible, the AI can track them.

Enter a Jersey Number

Just the number. The AI identifies and tracks that player automatically across the entire video — no manual annotation or tagging.
